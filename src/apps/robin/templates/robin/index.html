Congratulations on Your Engagement

You’ve set your wedding date. Now, are you ready to create a ceremony that touches the hearts of your guests and shows them the love you share? Celebrant Robin would be delighted to work with you
to create the ceremony of your dreams.

Our first meeting is a no-fee, no-obligation 30-minute session. Once we commit to working together, you will meet with Robin for a longer, in-depth session to share your story and begin the ceremony design.

Creating the Ceremony You Want

Together we will build your unique ceremony. We can interweave traditions from your past with new rituals and customs. As you describe your vision for your wedding, Celebrant Robin will identify the metaphors and themes of your ceremony, selecting readings, blessings, poems, music, and ritual elements that align with your vision. You might consider including a remembrance ceremony, a unity ritual, a ring blessing, or a family-joining ritual. Robin can assist you and your ceremony participants in writing vows or speeches, ensuring that everything flows.

As we develop the ceremony script, you will review each version and add your comments and suggestions. The week before your wedding date, we will finalize the ceremony and prepare it for presentation.

About Robin

Celebrant Robin officiates at weddings and other ceremonies in Philadelphia, PA, the suburbs of Montgomery, Bucks, and Delaware Counties and in neighboring New Jersey. A writer, theater director, and life-long learner, Robin brings all of her strengths and experience into creating professional, poetic, heartfelt ceremonies that delight her clients.

Robin holds Master’s degrees from the University of Pennsylvania and LaSalle University, along with certifications in ceremony development for weddings, funerals, and life-cycle events from the Celebrant Institute and Foundation. She was ordained as a non-denominational minister through the Universal Brotherhood Movement. Robin is also a registered civil celebrant in the State of New Jersey.

To learn more about Celebrant Robin, follow us on Facebook visit us on Wedding Wire, and read our Blog.

Why work
with Robin?

By working with Celebrant Robin, you benefit from her training in the elements of ritual, ceremony, myth, and transitions. If you want a ceremony that represents you as the individuals or couple you are, Celebrant Robin is your officiant.

The fee for Robin’s services includes all client meetings, travel costs within a 60 mile radius, creation of the final ceremony, and officiating and coordinating the ceremony elements. Following the wedding, Celebrant Robin will send your signed wedding license to the county Marriage Bureau and confirm its arrival. Payment options include check, cash, and PayPal.

In addition to wedding and elopement ceremonies, Celebrant Robin is available to officiate at baby namings, memorial services, vow renewals, and any other occasion where you want a ceremony to mark a transition or life event.
